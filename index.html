FAQ

Quick answers

Is Chiaru free?

Yes. Chiaru for desktop is completely free.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Your notes, flashcards, and quizzes are stored locally. You can export or import whenever you like. Only AI features use network, but we plan to add local AI usage in the future.

Can I import from Anki?

Chiaru supports Anki flashcard imports, and they are constantly being refined as Chiaru updates.
